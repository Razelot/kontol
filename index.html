More About Me


I grew up in a small island in Indonesia called "Bali". Being able to live at a tropical paradise was something which I took for granted when I was little. Some of the things that miss include riding my bike next to the beach and eating the local cuisine.


Sometimes I like to doodle during my free time. I've always enjoyed being able to express my imaginations in the form of pictures or drawings. Learning to manipulate digital images have opened new possibilities for me. I find that that coming up with eye catching designs pleases me and I sometimes take extra time to pay attention to finer details.


Playing video games is one of my favorite things to do and it also have affected my life in some way. It was the original motivation that made me want to learn how to program and also the source of some of my inspirations.
